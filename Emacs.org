* Shortcut keys
** Basic
*** C-x C-f Find a file(Open or New)
*** C-x C-s Save current file 
*** C-x C-c Quit 
*** C-x C-w Save as
** Important
*** C-g     Quit current command
*** C-x u   Undo last command
** Help
*** C-h f   Show function 
*** C-h v   Show variables
*** C-h k   Show shortcut keys
** Search
*** C-s     Search below
*** C-r     Search above
** Advanced
*** M-x linum-mode  Show or Hide line numbers 
*** C-x k           Close buffer
